Data analysis

Attacks coming from China

Two of the provinces - Beijing and Jiangsu - have a lot more attacks than others which might be connected to two big cities in these provinces - Pekin and Nankin. This might suggest that the attacks are a bit more more organised than simply one curious individual or the big cities just provide necessary technology and equipment in contrast to the countryside.



Attacks per hour


Post-lunch time in Poland seems like the prime time for hacking. Most of the attacks occur in the afternoon but it is hard to find a regular pattern.
